## Long (Website / Press-style)

**Title:** NRAPA Launches Secure Members Portal — Membership, Compliance & Motivation in One Place

**Date:** [Insert launch date]

**Intro**

The **National Rifle and Pistol Association (NRAPA)** has launched its **Members Portal** — a secure, SAPS-accredited platform that gives members a single place to manage their membership, certificates, firearm records, and compliance.

**Built for South African firearm owners**

The portal is built for hunters and sport shooters who need to stay on top of the Firearms Control Act, maintain dedicated status, and keep their documents and records in order — without the paperwork.

**Key features**

| Feature | What it does |
|--------|----------------|
| **Certificates & documents** | Download membership certificates, Dedicated Hunter/Sport Shooter certificates, proof of paid-up membership, and endorsement letters. Every document is **QR-verifiable** so SAPS, DFOs, or ranges can confirm authenticity instantly. |
| **Virtual Safe** | Digital firearm registry: calibres, makes, models, serials (barrel/frame/receiver), and licence details. Track barrel life and get reminders before licences expire. |
| **Virtual Loading Bench** | Store reload recipes (bullet, powder, primer, brass, COAL), track component inventory, run ladder tests, and calculate cost per round. Print load labels for the bench. |
| **Activity submission** | Log shooting and hunting activities. Build the activity record required for dedicated status and annual reporting (e.g. before 31 October). |
| **Endorsement requests** | Request endorsement letters for licence applications via a guided process. Issued letters are available in your document list and are QR-verifiable. |
| **Digital membership card** | QR-verifiable membership card for your phone — for the range, at the gate, or when asked by SAPS or an official. |
| **Learning Centre & knowledge tests** | Study material aligned to NRAPA’s official curriculum and take knowledge tests for Dedicated Hunter and Sport Shooter. |

**Compliance and security**

- **SAPS accredited** — NRAPA is accredited for Dedicated Hunter (FAR 1300127) and Dedicated Sport Shooter (FAR 1300122).
- **Secure access** — Members log in to a dedicated site; no sensitive data on the public website.
- **POPIA-aware** — Data handling and user deletion requests are supported in line with privacy requirements.

**Who it’s for**

- New members joining NRAPA and working towards dedicated status  
- Existing members who want certificates, endorsements, and activity tracking in one place  
- Reloaders who want to store recipes and print load labels  
- Anyone who wants a digital membership card and fewer paper documents  

**How to get started**

Visit **[members.nrapa.co.za]** to register or log in. Existing NRAPA members can use the portal as soon as they have an account; new members can join through the same site.

**Contact**

For membership or portal support, contact NRAPA via [info@nrapa.co.za] or through the main NRAPA website.
