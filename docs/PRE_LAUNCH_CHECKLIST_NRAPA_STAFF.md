# NRAPA Members Portal — Pre-Launch Task List (NRAPA Staff)

Tasks for **NRAPA staff** to complete before and around go-live. Tick each when done. If something does not apply, note “N/A” and why.

---

## 1. Business & operations

- [ ] **Membership types** — Confirm all packages (Basic, Hunter, Sport, Both, etc.) in the admin have correct names, prices, and renewal amounts.
- [ ] **Pricing** — Confirm sign-up and renewal amounts are correct; any upgrade fees (e.g. Basic + Hunter) add up as intended.
- [ ] **SAPS accreditation** — Confirm FAR numbers (1300122 Sport, 1300127 Hunting) with SAPS and that they match what is shown on the site and in communications.
- [ ] **Contact details** — Confirm support email (e.g. info@nrapa.co.za) and any phone/address on the site and in the portal are correct.
- [ ] **Support process** — Assign someone to answer member and portal queries (email/tickets) from launch day.

---

## 2. Domains & service provider

- [ ] **Domain access** — Obtain login/access to the domain registrar(s) for all NRAPA domains (e.g. nrapa.co.za, members subdomain).
- [ ] **Service provider change** — Decide on new hosting/DNS provider; plan changeover. Confirm who will make DNS/hosting changes and by when.
- [ ] **DNS cutover** — After go-live, confirm DNS for the members portal (and main site if moving) points to the new server; check propagation.

---

## 3. Member import

- [ ] **Import data** — Provide or approve the member data/file for import (as agreed with developer).
- [ ] **Double-check import** — After import is run: check a sample of members (names, membership types, contact details), verify total counts, and confirm no major errors. Sign off before go-live.

---

## 4. Content & documents (NRAPA sign-off)

- [ ] **Welcome / landing page** — Review and approve headlines, feature text, and CTAs.
- [ ] **Learning Centre & knowledge tests** — Double-check every article and knowledge test for accuracy, alignment with NRAPA curriculum, and correct answers. Sign off when satisfied.
- [ ] **Official documents — wording** — Double-check the **wording** of all official documents: membership certificate, dedicated status certificates, endorsement letters, welcome letter, and any standard letters. Approve wording before launch.
- [ ] **Official documents — layout** — Confirm **layout** of all official documents: letterhead, logos, positioning, fonts, margins, QR placement, borders or stamps. Approve final PDFs before launch.
- [ ] **Terms & conditions** — Finalise membership and portal terms; confirm the version in the system is the one you want shown at sign-up/activation.
- [ ] **Privacy / POPIA** — Finalise privacy notice or policy; confirm process for access/correction/deletion requests from members.

---

## 5. Compliance & legal

- [ ] **POPIA** — Confirm privacy notice is in place and you know how to handle access/correction/deletion requests.
- [ ] **Terms acceptance** — Confirm members must accept current terms where required and that version/date are recorded.
- [ ] **Data retention** — Decide how long you keep member and payment data and when/how to delete or anonymise.

---

## 6. Communications & go-live

- [ ] **Launch date** — Agree date and time for “portal is live” (and any maintenance window to communicate).
- [ ] **Launch ad / announcement** — Approve launch text (e.g. from [LAUNCH_AD_DRAFT.md](LAUNCH_AD_DRAFT.md)); confirm URLs, contact email, and FAR numbers in the ad are correct.
- [ ] **Channels** — Decide where to announce (email, website, social, etc.); schedule or prepare posts.
- [ ] **Existing members** — Prepare email or message to notify imported members: portal is live, how to log in (link), set or reset password, and where to get help.

---

## 7. Post-launch (first 24–48 hours)

- [ ] **Support** — Respond to first member queries; escalate any critical sign-up or login issues to the developer.
- [ ] **Verification** — Spot-check a few member certificates and QR verification links in the wild and report any issues.

---

*When all items that apply are complete, NRAPA is ready for launch from your side. Technical deployment and testing are covered in the developer checklist.*
